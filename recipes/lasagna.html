Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the comforting layers of our Classic Homemade Lasagna! This timeless Italian dish is a culinary masterpiece that combines silky sheets of pasta with rich, savory fillings.

Ingredients

  • Ground Veal
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sofrito
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Lasagna sheets
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
