Chicken Katsu

Description

Embark on a journey to Japan's culinary heart with our Crispy Chicken Katsu recipe, a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will transport your taste buds to a world of savory delight.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast Fillets
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Egg
  • Neutral Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Seasin the chicken and dredge in flour
  2. Coat each breast in egg and then press into the Panko
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown
