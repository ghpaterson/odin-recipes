Potato Dumplings

Description

Discover the rustic charm of our Savory Potato Dumplings, a hearty and satisfying dish that brings warmth to any table.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Bread Cubes
  • Grated Nutmeg
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Fresh Chives

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes for 15-20 minutes
  2. Fry the bread cubes in butter until crispy
  3. Mash the potatoes and season with the spices, add the eggs and flour and mix
  4. Shape the dough into small circle and indent the middle. Fill the middle with the crutons and shape into a ball
  5. Cook the balls in boiling water until the float to the surface
  6. Transfer to a plate and garnish with browned butter, chives and crutons
